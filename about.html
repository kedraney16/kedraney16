About the Art, Artists and Storefront Location

This page is meant to feature the newest artwork of an anonymous local artist, but more importantly, serve as a directory and compendium of information on as many prominent local artists as is possible. If you wish to view our featured artist's work in person, our primary retail location is now Artisan's Hand In Montpelier. Please Click here, in order to view their primary webpage and get information on address and retail storefront hours..
